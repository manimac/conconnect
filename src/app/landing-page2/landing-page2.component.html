About Us

Since 2020 ConConnect has revolutionized the process of reentry by connecting formerly incarcerated people with services instantly. ConConnect provides the justice-impacted community with resources and tools to make a successful Reentry plan and create a pathway towards professional employment or opportunities through networking and building professional connections.

Join the Community

A community of Professionals awaits you inside.

Reentry organizations, nonprofits, employers, mentors, advocates, you name it, they’re inside our community.

Jobs Around You

We work with employers in most industry’s to make sure you have options. Utilize your community and connections to create a pathway to professional employment.

ConAcademy

Learn and grow with our online video courses

Founder’s Note

ConConnect was made to inspire professionalism, make your Reentry easily accessible and provide you with the resources, community, and services for you to connect, learn and advance.
